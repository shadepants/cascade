1. GEOLOGICAL FOUNDATION
   * Fractal Elevation: Implemented 4-octave FBM (Fractal Brownian Motion) for
     natural, jagged terrain.
   * Tectonic Plates: Added Voronoi plate simulation; mountain ranges form at
     continental collisions, trenches at oceanic boundaries.
   * Moisture Transport: Implemented wind-driven climate simulation; prevailing
     winds create realistic rain shadows (deserts) behind mountain ranges.
2. DEEP HISTORY & RUINS
   * Ancient Era Simulation: Worldgen now simulates a 500-year "Ancient Era" that
     collapses before the modern game begins.
   * Fossilization: Extinct civilizations leave behind weathered Ruins (♜) on the
     map.
   * Legendary Artifacts: Ruins contain high-significance items (e.g., Shattered
     Crowns, Obsidian Seals) providing mythic targets for players.

  3. FACTION & LEADERSHIP LOGIC
   * Internal Interest Groups: Factions now contain competing coalitions
     (Merchant Guilds, High Commands) that shift faction ethics over time.
   * Ruler Traits: Leaders gain traits like 'Bloodthirsty', 'Industrious', or
     'Xenophobic' that mechanically alter war thresholds and economic growth.
   * Dynastic Succession: Added legitimacy stats and aging; ruler deaths trigger
     succession crises and potential civil war fractures.
4. DYNAMIC WORLD SYSTEMS
   * Strategic Resources: Added resource nodes (Iron, Gold, Relics) that act as
     geographic conflict magnets for recursive warfare.
   * Colonization: Factions now found new colonies every 20 years based on
     population pressure and wealth.
   * Settlement Growth: Towns now scale in size (1-5) and can be abandoned into
     ruins based on simulation stressors.

  5. HIGH-FIDELITY VISUALS
   * Painterly Rendering: Implemented Lambertian Hillshading for 3D depth and
     seeded Micro-Textures for a rich topographical feel.
   * Animated Entities: Replaced flat glyphs with animated procedural figures
     (capes, robes, heads) featuring idle bobbing and drop shadows.
   * Interactive Camera: Added full zoom support (+/- keys) and an animated
     atmospheric cloud/fog layer.
6. SCALE & PERFORMANCE
   * Map Size: Increased grid from 24x24 (576 tiles) to 128x128 (16,384 tiles).
   * Debug Visualization: Added 'M' key toggle to cycle between Normal, Elevation
     (Heightmap), and Moisture (Rainfall) views.
   * Technical Status: Zero-error build; optimized culling ensures high
     performance at 128x128 scale.
